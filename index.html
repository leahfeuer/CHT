Cobble Hill Therapy was started to provide a safe, objective, compassionate, and trusting environment for individuals to improve their lives and maximize their goals. In recognizing how experiences and thoughts inform our moods and behaviors, we can work toward desired change.

Hi, I'm Jessica Vogel.

I am a licensed psychotherapist in private practice, based in Downtown Brooklyn. I specialize in the treatment of anxiety, depression, perinatal issues, guilt, stress, life transitions, and interpersonal relationships. I also have extensive experience working with adolescents for stress management, coping skills, behavior management and trauma/PTSD.

I received my undergraduate degree in Psychology from Brown University and my Masters degree in Social Work from Columbia University. My approach to therapy is integrative, drawing largely from a Cognitive Behavioral Therapy perspective; however, I individually tailor each treatment plan according to your specific needs.
